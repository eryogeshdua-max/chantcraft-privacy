Privacy Policy – ChantCraft

Effective Date: November 14, 2025

1. Introduction

ChantCraft (“the App”) is designed to provide a peaceful, personalized chanting experience with full respect for your privacy. We collect only the minimum information required to operate our services and we ensure that your data stays safe and under your control.

2. Information We Collect

ChantCraft collects limited and essential data to ensure smooth functionality:

  • Device & Technical Data: Basic details such as device type, OS version, and crash logs for app optimization and debugging.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics such as session duration, feature usage, and performance metrics.
  • Optional Account Data: If you sign in using email or OTP for premium access, we collect your email or phone number strictly for authentication.
  • Chant Progress & Mala Counts: Stored locally on your device unless you enable cloud backup.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process the collected data to:

  • Enable app features and provide core chanting functionality
  • Improve performance, stability, and user experience
  • Deliver AI-powered features (only if you enable them manually)
  • Manage premium memberships, trials, and purchases
  • Detect misuse and maintain app security

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Your chants, mala counts, preferences, and progress remain stored on your device by default.
  • No personal chanting data is uploaded to our servers without your explicit action (e.g., backup or account login).
  • If cloud backup or login is used, data is stored securely in encrypted servers hosted by our service providers.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Google Analytics / Firebase: Anonymous usage analytics.
  • Google AI Studio: For AI-based recommendations (only if enabled).
  • Payment Processors: Google Play Billing for secure transactions.

These providers have their own privacy policies and comply with Google Play’s data safety requirements.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data sharing occurs only in these cases:

  • Anonymized analytics shared with service providers
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To prevent fraud or abuse of services

7. Permissions Used

The App may request permissions such as:

  • Vibration / Haptics: For bead feedback
  • Audio / Microphone: Only if you enable voice-based chants or AI recitation
  • Notifications: For reminders or chant goals

All permissions are optional and requested with clear user consent.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access data stored in your account
  • Delete your data from your device or cloud
  • Opt out of analytics (via settings)
  • Disable AI features at any time
  • Request full data removal by contacting us

9. Children's Privacy

ChantCraft is safe for all age groups. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

10. Security

We use modern security practices including encryption, secure authentication, and restricted data access. However, no digital system can guarantee 100% protection.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as needed for legal, operational, or feature-related reasons. Significant changes will be communicated within the App.

12. International Users

By using the App, you consent to the processing of your information in countries where our servers and partners operate, which may have different data protection laws.
