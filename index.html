Privacy Policy for ChantCraft

Effective Date: November 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ChantCraft. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

ChantCraft is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.
  • Chant Data: Your custom chants and mala counts are stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the ChantCraft app functionality
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand how users interact with the app

4. Data Storage

All your personal chants, mala counts, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not store this data on external servers unless you explicitly choose to back up your data using cloud services.

5. Third-Party Services

ChantCraft may integrate with third-party services such as:

  • Google AI Studio: For AI-powered features (if enabled)
  • Analytics Services: To understand app usage patterns

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytical purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Opt-out of data collection where applicable
  • Request information about how your data is used

8. Children's Privacy

ChantCraft is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app or via email.

11. International Users

ChantCraft is available globally. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your information to servers that may be located outside your country of residence.
