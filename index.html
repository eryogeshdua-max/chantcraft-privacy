Privacy Policy for ChantCraft

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ChantCraft – Japp Counter ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, secure environment for your spiritual practice. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

In Short: We prioritize your privacy. We do not sell your data, we do not record your voice, and we store the majority of your progress locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect & How We Use It

We adhere to data minimization principles. We only collect the data necessary for the App’s functionality.

A. Data Stored Locally (On Your Device)

The following data is stored securely on your device and is not automatically uploaded to our servers unless you choose to back it up (if applicable) or interact with online features:

  • App Progress: Chanting counts, current levels, unlocked gems, and streaks.
  • Preferences: App settings, customizations, and background choices.

B. Data Processed for Authentication & Purchases

To manage your subscription and verify unique users without collecting personal identities, we utilize:

  • Firebase Authentication: We use anonymous authentication tokens and device UUIDs to securely manage your session. We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Purchase Tokens: If you purchase a subscription or item, we process verification tokens provided by Google Play Billing to validate the transaction. We do not have access to your credit card or banking details.

3. Sensitive Permissions & Feature Usage

Google Play requires explicit explanations for sensitive permissions.

Microphone (Record Audio)

  • Usage: Used strictly for the "Voice Mode" feature to detect chanting via speech-to-text analysis.
  • Privacy Guarantee: Audio is processed in real-time within the device's volatile memory (RAM). We do not record, save, store, or transmit your voice audio to any external server. Once chanting is detected, the audio data is immediately discarded.

Physical Activity / Sensors (Proximity & Light)

  • Usage: Used for "Pocket Mode" to turn off the screen or detect handling during practice to save battery.
  • Privacy Guarantee: Sensor data is processed locally and never leaves your device.

Storage (Read/Write)

  • Usage: Required to save your progress, user settings, and custom background images.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party service providers to facilitate basic app functions. These providers have their own privacy policies which adhere to strict data protection standards.

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure, anonymous authentication.
  • Google Play Services: Used for handling in-app purchases and subscriptions.

We DO NOT use:

  • Advertising SDKs (e.g., AdMob)
  • Marketing Analytics
  • Social Media Trackers
  • Location Tracking

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local Data: You have full control. You can delete your local progress at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the App.
  • Server Data: Because our authentication is anonymous, we do not hold a permanent profile linked to your real-world identity. Verification tokens on our servers are retained only as long as necessary to validate active subscriptions or prevent fraud, and are deleted automatically after 180 days of inactivity.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our App is designed to be family-friendly. We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families Policy.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
  • The App does not contain interest-based advertising or external links unsuitable for children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the latest revisions were made. We advise you to review this page periodically.
