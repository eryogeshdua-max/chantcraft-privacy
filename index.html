Privacy Policy for ChantCraft – Japp Counter

Last Updated: December 25, 2025
Updated policy can be found at: https://eryogeshdua-max.github.io/chantcraft-privacy/

1. Introduction

Welcome to ChantCraft – Japp Counter. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use our app.

In simple words:
  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not record or store your voice
  • Most of your data stays only on your device

2. What Information We Collect and Why

We collect only the minimum information required for the app to function properly.

A. Data Stored on Your Device

  • Chant counts and progress
  • Levels, gems, and streaks
  • App settings and customizations
  • Background and display preferences

B. Authentication and Purchases

  • Firebase Anonymous Authentication: Uses anonymous tokens and device identifiers. No name, email, or phone number is collected.
  • Google Play Purchase Tokens: Used only to verify subscriptions or purchases. Payment details are never accessed or stored.

3. App Permissions and Their Use

Microphone (Record Audio)

  • Why: Used only for Voice Mode to detect chanting.
  • Privacy Promise: Audio is processed instantly on your device and deleted immediately. It is never recorded, stored, or sent to any server.

Sensors (Proximity & Light)

  • Why: Pocket Mode and battery optimization.
  • Privacy Promise: Sensor data never leaves your device.

Storage Access

  • Why: To save progress, settings, and custom backgrounds.

4. Third-Party Services Used

  • Google Firebase: Anonymous authentication
  • Google Play Services: Subscriptions and purchases

We do NOT use:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Marketing or analytics tools
  • Social media trackers
  • Location tracking

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your Device Data: Can be deleted anytime by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • Server Data: Anonymous verification data is automatically deleted after 180 days of inactivity.

6. Children’s Privacy

  • No personal data is knowingly collected from children under 13.
  • No ads or unsafe external links are shown.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
